About Me

Hi, I’m Yoshua, a student at Fontys. I was from Aruba and currently pursuing my passion for ICT. My academic journey started at Colegio San Nicolas (now called Colegio Nigel Matthews), where I completed HAVO, the Dutch equivalent of high school I guess. My interest in computers began back in MAVO, and by the time I reached HAVO, I knew this was the path I wanted to follow. ICT was the only subject that genuinely sparked my curiosity and kept me motivated to learn more.
Outside of school, I enjoy playing games, hitting the gym, and drawing (even if I’m not the best at it). I’m proud to say I’ve already lost over 20kg since last year, and I’m still working on my fitness goals. Drawing is more of a fun hobby for something I do to relax and let my creativity flow.
